Charlotte's Fudge Recipe

Instructions

  1. Line a baking tin with baking paper and have some iced water near you before you start.
  2. Melt any chocolate, condensed milk and sugar.
  3. Stir, stir, stir - aim for about 10-20 minutes until the mixture is thick and fudge like.
    • If you stop stirring at any point you will get a gritty consistency.
    • The consistency should go from shiny and liquid, to thick and matte.
    • For the final 5 minutes or so beat the mix together so it thickens up.
    • You can test your mixture by dropping half a teaspoon into iced water. If it hits the bottom of the container and goes like a toffee penny (kind of solid and sticky), you are on track.
  4. Then mix in any flavourings or toppings.
  5. Turn out into a tin.
    • Any tin will do, you can cut the fudge to the size that you wish.
    • Fudge sets quicker if you put it in a shallow tin.
    • Leave on the side or in the fridge if you are in a hurry until the fudge has set hard.

Ingredients

Chocolate Fudge

  • 1 can condensed milk
  • 400g chocolate
  • 25g butter
  • 100g icing sugar

Optional Ingredients

  • 55g of nuts, fruit or sweets
  • 1 tsp of flavouring - mint or orange tastes good

Christmas Fudge

  • 1/2 can condensed milk
  • 340g white chocolate
  • 1 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 3/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp all spice
  • 1/2 tsp ground cloves

Optional Ingredients

  • 55g of nuts, fruit or sweets - cranberries and walnuts work well

Boozy Fudge

  • 1 1/2 tins condensed milk
  • 600g golden caster sugar
  • 200g salted butter
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla paste (vanilla extract will also be fine)
  • 3 tbsp of your favourite liquor
